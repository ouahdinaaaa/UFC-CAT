About the company


UFCAT introduces a groundbreaking concept of meme combat using Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) on the Solana blockchain.

Through the integration of NFTs, each meme character will possess distinct attributes, strengths, and weaknesses, allowing for strategic gameplay and engagement.

These attributes will be algorithmically generated, ensuring fairness.

The combat system will enable users to engage their meme NFTs against each other in battles, where the outcome is determined by the community voting.

Holders of NFT will be able to earn CRO.

By leveraging the power of NFTs and blockchain technology, Ultimate Fighting Cat aims to revolutionize the memescoin space on the Solana blockchain, offering a new dimension of entertainment, creativity, and value to users worldwide.
