About us


Ultimate Fighting Cat (UFCAT) introduces a groundbreaking concept of meme combat using Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) on the Base blockchain.

Through the integration of NFTs, each meme character will possess distinct attributes, strengths, and weaknesses, allowing for strategic gameplay and engagement.

These attributes will be algorithmically generated, ensuring fairness.

The combat system will enable users to engage their meme NFTs against each other in battles, where the outcome is determined by community voting.

Holders of NFTs will be able to earn ETH.

By leveraging the power of NFTs and blockchain technology, Ultimate Fighting Cat aims to revolutionize the memescoin space on the Base blockchain, offering a new dimension of entertainment, creativity, and value to users worldwide.
